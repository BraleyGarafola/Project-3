3D Cubism

This piece was one of my favorites to do because I was able to bring a cubism image to life. This image is of Picasso's "Cubist Guitar". I not only chose this image for its colors, but I chose the image because I love music myself. The use of cardboard was a great medium to use to keep the foundation of the piece structurally stable. And I chose different parts of the original image and made four 3D parts of the image and stacked them on top of one another to complete the process. And the use of paints helped in bring the cubism image to life.
