Object Iteration Spreads

Any artistic style that could be thought of, was used to create artwork for and from a single item. I chose grapes for my object and had to create multiple artistic iterations from them. From Indian Ink and Acrylic to the use of the grapes and vines itself, I got to incorporate physical artwork into the digital styles that were used to create a color-popping and eye-catching book.
