Graphic Response Posters

By only using black and white, I got to create graphic response posters that could only be designed from real life news article stories. Each poster holds an important meaning that comes from within the news arctiles written. I had full artistic control over my poster choices as long as they were clean and easily legible. Researching for this particular concept was a real eye opening experience.
