Christmas Tree Cake Cereal

The concept of these pieces were to come up with a cereal brand and to create a box with an ad for the cereal. One of the most popular Little Debbie Snacks is their Christmas Tree Cakes, so I chose to make it a cereal. I was restricted to one Pantone color for the whole piece. And due to copyright reasons, instead of my product being owned by “Little Debbie”, it is owned by “Little DebHEE-HEE” ft. Michael Jackson.
