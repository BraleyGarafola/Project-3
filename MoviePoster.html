The Goonies Movie Poster

With the choice being given to me mysteriously, I got to choose 3 spot colors and create a movie poster based on an iconic 80s movie. The catch was that I was also mysteriously given an art style. For this particular piece, I was given “The Goonies” with the art style of Art Deco.
